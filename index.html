My Projects

I have worked on various projects and works related to humanitarian based for the development of environment
I have won prizes for some of the projects

Object Detection System - 2019

This project was successfully completed for the safeguard of helmets in the rental bikes in the city of Bengaluru, as there were many theft or simply carry away of helmets by the customers. Our product was made using Arduino and RFID Sensors to detect the helmet, if detected then it ends the ride else if not found it sends an alert message to the respective rental service headquarters.

Save Birds project - (2020 - present)

This project is carried on an environment reason as many birds are affected by the cell towers radiation and this causes huge life damage to them and thair young eggs.Our product's main idea is to check the frequency bandwidth of all the cell towers in the city and if it goes beyound the required frequency then the product will immediately report to the Cell Phone Head Quarters for eg: Airtel, Jio, etc

All Weather Wildfire Detection Security Drone System - (2020 - present)

This project's main idea is to save the forests from the wildfire happening worldwide. Our drone will detect the fire at the early stage and locates the location of that specific area and then it will report to the nearby foreat office. We are using an image processing algorithm for fire detection and using the GPS Sensors and Cameras of the Rpi, the drone clicks some pictures and sends to the nearby office
